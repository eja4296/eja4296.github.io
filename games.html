Audio Attack

Description: Audio Attack is a "music-generated," or "music-reactive" game. To play, all you have to do is stream music from your computer (from Spotify, Pandora, YouTube, etc.), and the audio is analyzed and creates the game components.

Team Size: 1

Date: Summer 2018

Role: This project was made during the University of Rochester Computation Methods for Understanding Music, Media, and Minds NSF REU program. I worked with Professor Sreepathi Pai to create this prototype game based on research I conducted on music-generate games. I also wrote a paper, gave a lightning talk, and created a poster for a final presentation.

Languages/Programs/Software: Processing

Link to poster: Poster Presentation

Link to paper: Research Paper
