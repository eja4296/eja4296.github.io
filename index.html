About Me

Hi, I'm Erik Azzarano! I am a fourth year student at Rochester Institute of Technology in Rochester, New York. I am a Game Design and Development Major with a Secondary Major in Psychology. I am originally from the Philadelphia area in Pennsylvania and live there when I am not at school.

I love making and playing games and other forms of interactive media! My goal is to have a career where I can combine the principles of both Game Development and Psychology to create the most fun and beneficial interactive experiences using my technological and psychological skills.
