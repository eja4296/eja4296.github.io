Interactive Environments

Prison Ship Level

Description: The Prison Ship Level is a 3D explorable environment based in a flying prison ship.

Team Size: 4

Date: Spring 2016

Role: Created and textured several models including the steering wheel, ladder, and ship itself. Took all of the models that the team made and created the actual level inside of Unity. Also implemented lighting effects such as the user-held lantern and fire in the engine room, as well as animation effects such as the ship bobbing up-and-down to appear as if it is flying.

Languages/Programs/Software: Unity, Maya, C#, Adobe Photoshop

Kirby Interactive Environment

Description: The Kirby Interactive Environment is a 3D explorable environment with autonomous agents.

Team Size: 1

Date: Spring 2016

Role: Using assets found from online resources, created autonomous agents that moved around the environment using different steering forces. The steering forces include path following, wandering, pursing, and arriving.

Languages/Programs/Software: Unity, C#, Maya, Adobe Photoshop
