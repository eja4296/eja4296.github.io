User Interfaces

Top Minds

Description: The user interface for an original mobile and tablet application known as Top Minds.

Team Size: 4

Date: Spring 2016

Role: As a team, we went through the process of designing a user interface for a mobile/tablet app. The process included creating moodboards, personas, use cases and scenarios, a functional specification model, storyboarding, sketches and wireframes for a few of the app screens, and rough and final mockups for those screens. Upon completion of all individual components, a booklet was created to hold all of them and show the entire design process.

Languages/Programs/Software: Adobe Photoshop
